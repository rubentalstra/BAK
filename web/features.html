Explore BAK Tracker's Key Features

Invite-Only Membership

Join exclusive student associations with unique invite codes, ensuring privacy and exclusivity. Keep your membership secure and organized within the app.

Multiple Associations

Easily manage memberships across multiple associations, and switch between them seamlessly. Stay connected with all of your interactions in one place.

Bak Tracking

Track, send, and approve drink debts (bakken) with just a few taps. The app provides real-time tracking of your drink debts, making it easy to settle them.

Leaderboards & Competitions

Stay competitive with real-time leaderboards that rank users based on both bak debts and bak consumption. Organize fun competitions and bets with your friends.

Bets

Create and track bets with other members. Settle who owes the most by placing bets, and track the winners and losers automatically within the app.

Profile Management

Update your personal information, including your display name and profile picture. Easily customize your profile to reflect your identity within your associations.

Role-Based Permissions

Assign roles such as "Voorzitter," "Penningmeester," and more, with custom permissions for each user. This allows for effective delegation and role-based control in your associations.

Deep Linking

Automatically join an association when you click on an invite link shared by other members. Deep linking makes it simple to onboard new members seamlessly.

How BAK Tracker Benefits You

Efficiency

BAK Tracker simplifies the process of tracking drink debts and settling them in real-time. No more confusion over who owes what – everything is tracked seamlessly.

Community Engagement

Encourage friendly competitions and bets among members. BAK Tracker promotes engagement within your association by fostering interaction and participation.

Customization

With role-based permissions and profile management, you can personalize the app to meet your association’s needs. Control who can invite members, track drink debts, and manage competitions.
